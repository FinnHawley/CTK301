About the Artist

My name is Finn Hawley, and I am a creative young designer from St. Louis, Missouri. I am passionate about many art forms, however my primary interests tend to fall under the technological side of things. I am studying Interdisciplinary Technologies at Illinois State University in Normal, Illinois. My major is part of a greater program called Creative Technologies that focuses on web design, graphic design, music production, video game production, UI/UX, and even VR design. My major focuses on web design and graphic design. I am currently taking CTK 301: Web Design and Development, and I am having a lot of fun. I can definitely tell that this is something I could see myself doing the rest of my life.
